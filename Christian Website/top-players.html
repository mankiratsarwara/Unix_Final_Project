Season Leaders - Top 5 Players

#1

Stephen Curry

Point Guard

Drafted in : 2009 by the Golden State Warriors

Currently averaging: 31.9 PTS, 5.7 AST, 5.5 TRB

Awards & honors (some notable awards):

  • 3x NBA champion: 2015, 2017, 2018
  • 2x Most Valuable Player: 2015, 2016 (the only unanimous MVP selection in league history (2016)
  • 7x NBA All-Star
  • 6x All-NBA selection
  • 5× NBA three-point field goals leader: 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017

Brief description:

If you have heard of basketball, you have heard of Stephen Curry. Son of the NBA veteran sharpshooter Dell curry and born in Akron Ohio, Curry is argued to be one of the greatest NBA shooters of all time. Curry has been loyal to his team since the day he got drafted.

#2

Bradley Beal

Shooting Guard

Drafted in : 2012 by the Washington Wizards

Currently averaging: 31.4 PTS, 4.4 AST, 4.7 TRB

Awards & honors (some notable awards):

  • 3× NBA All-Star: 2018, 2019, 2021
  • NBA All-Rookie Team

Brief description:

Born in St Louis, Missouri, Bradley Beal had come a long way. He is currently averaging 30 points for the second time in his career. In the beginning of his career, he played with John Wall, but now he is playing with the athletic player, Russell Westbrook.

#3

Joel Embiid

Center

Drafted in : 2014 by the Philadelphia 76ers

Currently averaging: 29.2 PTS, 2.9 AST, 10.7 TRB

Awards & honors (some notable awards):

  • 4x NBA All-Star
  • 2× All-NBA Second Team: 2018, 2019
  • 2× NBA All-Defensive Second Team: 2018, 2019

Brief description:

Drafted in 2014, Embiid only started playing 2 seasons after due to a foot injury. He was born in Cameroon, but did not touch a basketball until the age of 15. He would usually play soccer and volleyball, where he had dreams to play professional volleyball in Europe.

#4

Damian Lillard

Point Guard

Drafted in : 2012 by the Portland Trail Blazers

Currently averaging: 28.7 PTS, 7.6 AST, 4.3 TRB

Awards & honors (some notable awards):

  • 6x NBA All-Star
  • All-NBA First Team: 2018
  • NBA Rookie of the Year: 2013
  • 3× All-NBA Second Team: 2016, 2019, 2020

Brief description:

NBA superstar on the court, and rapper off the court, Damian Lillard is considered one of the most underrated players of our generation. Coming from Oakland, Lillard is a sharpshooter that could do it all. He hold the record of the fastest player to score 10,000 points in the NBA.

#5

Giannis Antetokounmpo

Power Forward

Drafted in : 2013 by the Milwaukee Bucks

Currently averaging: 28.2 PTS, 5.9 AST, 10.9 TRB

Awards & honors (some notable awards):

  • 2× NBA Most Valuable Player: 2019, 2020
  • 5× NBA All-Star
  • 2× All-NBA First Team: 2019, 2020
  • NBA Defensive Player of the Year: 2020
  • NBA Most Improved Player: 2017

Brief description:

The "Greak Freak" is another player that came a long way. When he was younger, Giannis remembers when his parents were struggling to support their family. In his NBA carreer, as a rookie, Giannis was averaging 6.8 points. Improving his game each year, Giannis is now averaging 28.2 points and signed a five-year contract worth $228 million!
